Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How can I book a shipment?

You can book a shipment easily through our online booking form on the Booking page. Simply fill in the sender and receiver information, shipment details, and submit the form. You'll receive an immediate confirmation with a tracking number.

How do I track my shipment?

Visit our Tracking page and enter your tracking number (e.g., PAN-LX12345-ABC) to view the current status and location of your shipment. You'll also receive email updates at each milestone.

What services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive logistics services including Air Freight, Sea Freight, Land Transport, Warehousing & Storage, Customs Clearance, and Supply Chain Solutions. Learn more on our Services page.

How long does international shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on the destination and service selected:

  • Air Freight: 3-7 business days
  • Sea Freight: 14-45 business days
  • Express Air: 1-2 business days
Do you provide insurance for shipments?

Yes, we offer comprehensive cargo insurance to protect your shipments against loss or damage. Insurance can be added during the booking process. Contact our team for insurance rates and coverage options.

What customs documentation do I need?

Required documents vary by shipment type and destination, but typically include: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading or Airway Bill, Certificate of Origin, and any necessary permits or licenses. Our customs team can assist you with documentation requirements.

Can I schedule a pickup?

Absolutely! During the booking process, you can select your preferred pickup date and time. We offer flexible scheduling, including same-day pickup for urgent shipments in the Greater Toronto Area.

How do I get a quote?

You can request a quote by filling out our booking form or by contacting us directly. For large or recurring shipments, we offer customized pricing. Our team typically responds within 24 hours.
