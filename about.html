Our Story

Founded in 2005, Pan Logistics has grown from a small local trucking company to one of Canada's leading logistics and freight forwarding providers. Our journey has been defined by a commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

What started as a vision to revolutionize the Canadian logistics industry has evolved into a comprehensive network of services spanning air, sea, and land transportation, warehousing, and supply chain solutions.

Today, we are proud to serve over 2,500 clients across 85 countries, handling more than 50,000 shipments annually. Our success is built on the trust our clients place in us and our dedication to delivering exceptional service every time.

Our Journey

Key milestones that have shaped Pan Logistics into what it is today

2005

Company Founded

Pan Logistics was established in Toronto with a fleet of 5 trucks and a vision to transform Canadian logistics.

2008

Expanded to Freight Forwarding

Launched air and sea freight services, marking our entry into international logistics.

2012

First Warehouse Facility

Opened our first 50,000 sq ft warehouse in Mississauga to meet growing demand.

2015

Cross-Border Operations

Expanded services to include USA-Canada cross-border trucking and customs brokerage.

2018

Technology Investment

Launched advanced tracking systems and automated warehouse management platform.

2020

National Network

Extended operations to all major Canadian provinces with 10+ service centers.

2024

Global Reach

Established partnerships in 85+ countries, handling 50,000+ annual shipments.
